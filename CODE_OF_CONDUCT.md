# Code of Conduct

We are committed to creating a welcoming, inclusive, and
harassment-free environment for everyone, regardless of age,
body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression,
level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation. We believe that
diversity strengthens our work and that every contributor deserves
to be treated with dignity and respect.

## Statement on Diversity and Inclusivity

All community members are expected to use welcoming and inclusive
language, be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences,
gracefully accept constructive criticism, focus on what is best for
the community, and show empathy and kindness toward other community
members. We recognize that team members have different class schedules,
family obligations, and work commitments, and we are committed to
remaining flexible and accommodating of each other's availability.

## Expected and Unacceptable Behaviour

**Expected Behaviour:**

We expect members to uphold the following standards:

* Be inclusive and welcoming
* Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
* Be considerate of others' time and availability
* Respect boundaries
* Maintain professionalism in all project-related communication
* Communicate openly and thoughtfully, providing project updates to
  the team as they occur
* Contribute fairly and equitably to the project — each team member is
  expected to complete their assigned core tasks for every milestone
* Support fellow team members with their tasks when possible
* Give credit accordingly and acknowledge contributions from all members
* Be responsive to team communications and participate in scheduled
  weekly meetings

**Unacceptable Behaviour:**

The following behaviours are considered unacceptable:

* The use of sexualized language or imagery
* Personal attacks
* Trolling or insulting/derogatory comments
* Public or private harassment
* Publishing others' private information
* Failing to complete assigned tasks without communicating blockers or
  difficulties to the team in a timely manner
* Any other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate
  in a professional or academic setting

## Consequences for Unacceptable Behaviour

Community members who violate this code of conduct may face consequences
including a formal warning, temporary suspension from the project, or
permanent removal from the project, depending on the severity and
frequency of the violation. All consequences will be determined at the
discretion of the project maintainers. Repeated failure to meet agreed-upon
responsibilities may be escalated to the course instructional team.

## Procedure for Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, please report it by
contacting the project team. All reports will be reviewed and investigated
promptly and confidentially. If the issue cannot be resolved within the
team, it may be escalated to the DSCI 310 instructional team for further
guidance.
